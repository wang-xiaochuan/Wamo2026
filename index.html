> 缘起 / Genesis_Log

古希腊的英雄奥德修斯，在海路被封锁之际，
在喀耳刻的建议中进入冥界寻求指引。

When the seas were sealed, Odysseus entered the Underworld.
He had to enter the realm of the dead, for the world of the living could no longer guide him.

今天，我们站在一个类似的时刻。
技术不再只决定生死，而开始接管判断、重塑关系。

Today, we stand at a similar juncture.
Tech no longer just decides life; it takes over judgment and reshapes relationships.

只有越过这条冥河，才能获得一线生机。
这不是承诺彼岸的救赎，而是承认此岸已无法维持。

"Only by crossing the Styx can we find survival."
Not a promise of salvation, but an admission that this shore is unsustainable.

我们被迫进入边界地带，去询问那些已经付出代价的存在。
那些关于天堂的幻想，制度、理想和乌托邦。

Forced into the liminal zone, to question the buried past.
The 20th century's institutions, ideals, and utopias.
